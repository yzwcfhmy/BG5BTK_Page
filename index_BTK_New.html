关于我的故事

这里是BG5BTK，很高兴与您相识。


小时候，我总是蹲在爷爷的收音机旁，陶醉在那奇妙的声音中。电波穿越时空，将遥远的声音带到我的耳边，让我沉浸其中。

那些声音，仿佛是一阵轻柔的风，在我的内心轻抚。

当时的我，并不明白它们的来源，但它们给我带来的快乐和好奇心，却成为我童年时光中最美的记忆。

高中时代，每当夜幕降临，我都会打开收音机，调到FM95.2频道，倾听着那温暖的节目。

寂静的夜晚，我和这个频道成了最亲密的伙伴。渐渐地，我发现无线电世界的魅力。

它不仅仅是一种通信方式，更是一种沟通灵魂的方式。通过电波，我能与远方的人们分享情感、传递思念，这使我在高中时代找到了情感的寄托。

毕业后，我意识到自己需要去实现那个曾经的心愿——获得业余无线电执照。

我花费了半个月学习相关知识，终于如愿以偿。掌握了执照，我心潮澎湃，仿佛翻开了通往未知世界的大门。

我开始使用自己的设备，与其他业余爱好者交流心得，分享经验。

在这个庞大的无线电社区中，我结识了一群志同道合的朋友，我们相互学习、探索，一起追求着无线电之美。

未来，我将继续前行，用我的设备，与其他人交流。我愿意倾听世界的声音，用电波传递真情实感。

在这广阔而神秘的无线电世界中，电波承载着我的声音在空中自由飞翔，激荡着内心的激情与热血。

About My Story

This is BG5BTK, nice to meet you.


When I was a child, I used to squat beside my grandfather's radio and revel in its marvelous sounds. The radio waves traversed through time and space, bringing distant voices to my ears, and immersing me in them.

Those sounds were like a gentle breeze that caressed my heart.

At that time, I didn't understand their source, but the happiness and curiosity they brought me became the most beautiful memories of my childhood.

In high school, whenever the night fell, I would turn on the radio and tune into FM95.2 to listen to its warm programs. On silent nights, this channel became my closest companion. Gradually, I discovered the charm of the radio world.

It wasn't just a means of communication, but also a way of communicating souls. Through radio waves, I could share emotions and convey longings with people far away, which gave me emotional sustenance during my high school years.


After graduation, I realized that I needed to fulfill that long-cherished wish - to obtain an amateur radio license.

It took me half a month to learn the necessary knowledge, and finally, my wish came true. With the license in hand, I was overwhelmed with excitement, as if I had opened the door to an unknown world.

I started using my own equipment to exchange ideas and share experiences with other amateur enthusiasts.

In this vast radio community, I met a group of like-minded friends. We learned from each other, explored together, and pursued the beauty of radio waves.


In the future, I will continue to move forward and communicate with others using my equipment. I am willing to listen to the world's voice and transmit real feelings through radio waves.

In this vast and mysterious radio world, my voice is carried freely in the air by radio waves, stirring up my passion and blood.
